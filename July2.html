Khammam: In an alarming development, eight new positive cases of covid-19 were reported at Nelakondapalli in the district on Sunday. It was the first instance in the district wherein eight coronavirus cases were reported on a single day. Those who tested positive for coronavirus were primary contacts of a 44-year-old steel trader of Nelakondapalli who tested positive on Friday. According to a government press release here, three family members of the trader and five persons who work in his shop have contracted the virus.
